Meet Becks

Hi, I'm Becks. As a midwife with many years of experience in some of London's busiest tertiary maternity units, I've had the privilege of supporting countless families through the joys and challenges of pregnancy and birth. Now undertaking an MSc in Women's Health, I'm passionate about combining my clinical expertise with the latest research to provide thoughtful, reassuring care tailored to each woman's unique journey.

Planning to become a parent or talking about your previous birth experience and how it has affected your decisions for a future pregnancy are topics that can often feel daunting. Sometimes questions you may have feel too private to discuss in an antenatal class or too big to unpack in a maternity appointment.

I founded The Birth Tailor to provide tailored support and guidance for wherever you may be in your pregnancy journey. I'm committed to offering clear, balanced information alongside emotional support, ensuring you feel calm, confident, and ready for every possibility your birth journey may bring.

While I'm not involved in clinical tasks, I specialise in offering personalised advice to help you feel empowered every step of the way.
